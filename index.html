our story

The little red cart at the end of the driveway

Park on the Catch The Fire lot, follow the smell of the grill, and you'll find the red Attwell Food cart that's been feeding the neighbourhood since 1994.

It's nothing fancy — and that's the point. Good sausages and dogs, hand-cut fries, fair prices, and a friendly face. Everything's made to order while you wait.
