Our Story

A hidden gem at the end of the driveway

Tucked on the Catch The Fire lot in Etobicoke, Attwell Food is the little hotdog stand the neighbourhood keeps coming back to — the kind of spot that's almost too good to be true.

Everything's hot, fresh and made to order, with quality ingredients and honest prices. Quick, friendly, and worth the trip on your lunch break.

Made fresh, to order No heat lamps, no shortcuts — your food is cooked when you order it. That's why regulars rave about it.
