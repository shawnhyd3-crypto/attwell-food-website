Our Story

A friendly bite at the end of the driveway

Tucked at the end of the driveway on the Catch The Fire property, Attwell Food is the kind of spot the neighbourhood is glad to have — quick, affordable, and run by people who genuinely care.

We keep it simple: good food at a fair price, served with a smile. No fuss, no gimmicks — just a great lunch that leaves room in your wallet.

Good food for a good cause The owners give back — proceeds help support efforts to feed children overseas. Lunch here helps further than your table.
